School Admission

Parants/guardian should register their children/ wards on application form enclosed at the "Application" button. All the requirements mentioned in the registration form must be stricttly followed.
As proposed by State education department the age at the time of admission to varios classes should ordinarily not exceed.
Play group 3 years
Nursery 4 years
K.G 5 years
PClass I 6 years
Class II 7 years
Class III 8 years
One class will be added every year.
Transfer certificate and mark sheet from the last school attended, certified copy of municipal Birth Certificate should accompany the admission form.
School will finally be affiliated to CBSE – New Delhi

FEE REGULATIONS

Registration Fees - Rs 1500/-
Admin Fees - Rs 3000/-
Security - Rs 1500/-
Monthly Fees - Rs 1300/-
Fees are payable upto the 10th of every month failing which late fee fine is levied.
Books & School uniform shall be provided by the school and separately.

CONCESSIONS

If both brother & sister read in the school, concession of Rs 100/- will be given to one.

FOOD

Nourishing & wholesome mid-day meal shall be provided at the cost of Rs 150/- per month to the children.

TUTION

No extra tuitions will be allowed in the school.
Exceptionally weak students shall be given extra time by their own teachers.
Parent teacher meet shall be held every second Saturday of the month.
Annual Medical & Dental checkup of children shall be arranged by the school.

THE SCHOOL CURRICULUM

The course of study includes English, Computer Studies, Mathematics, History, Science, Local Languages Music Art & Physical Education.
Our teachers are very sensitive to individuals learning differences and needs, always innovating foe a better future. The School is designed with spacious Class Rooms, library facilities, playing school for Kindergarten Class and play ground amidst serene and calm atmosphere right in the heart of the town.
We strive for a home away from home where children would prepare for tough competition that lies ahead at the fundamental level.

EXTRA CURRICULAR ACTIVITIES

In extra curricular activities the school encourages students in their hobbies, singing , music and dramatics. Parents must also encourage their children to develop hobbies. These help in breaking the monotony of studies.

GENERAL DISCIPLINE

The school believes in discipline and good manners, tries and inculcates a sense of responsibility. Discipline distinguishes an upright person. We tried to instill discipline in the routine life of our students. In this regard certain rules are enforced.
Students are not allowed to bring valuables in school.
Expensive Jewelry is not allowed to be worn by any student.
All children must wear proper uniform, which includes proper shoes also.
Fees must be paid in time.
Students are not allowed to leave the school during school timings, except during an emergency.
Co-operation of parents in these matters is of paramount importance, which is, therefore, expected and requested.
